Our Story

We say the story of how we ve done it

From a small family kitchen to a cozy corner in Milan, our recipes were handed down at Sunday tables, perfected over years of love and patience. We knead by hand, simmer slow, and taste twice. Every plate is a memory brought to life and shared with you.

Flour, water, time, and care are our pillars. We source simple ingredients and let them shine without noise. This is how we ve done it and how we keep doing it, one warm plate at a time.
